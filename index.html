What's the 'Sitch?

Social Superhero was created for children with autism and the professionals who work with them. The app serves as a trusty sidekick, easily accessible when social situations seem insurmountable. Autism professionals can Create, Read, Update, and Delete situations, tailored to each child.

Version 2.0

In the next version the version I will add security and secure log in. The child will have access to the 'sitch's but they will be read only. The Autism professional will have an Adminstrative log-in. They will be able to have full control over setting the child's situations for a given week.

The Tech

  • MongoDB
  • Express
  • Node.js
  • React
  • JavaScript
  • Custom CSS

Find Where to Watch YOUR Team

Fan Watch was created in order for displaced fans to know where to go watch their team. There are a lot of factors that are important to a hardcore fans like -- will the sound be on? do lots of fans gather? is the food good?

Version 2.0

In the next version the user will be able to search for their team in any city, not just Atlanta. Also, it will be open to every team and leauge, beyond just the NFL.

The Tech

  • React
  • Ruby
  • Rails
  • Postgresql
  • Server-side calls to Yelp API
  • Custom CSS
